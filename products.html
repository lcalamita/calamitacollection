Vintage Crystal Wine Stoppers

I source the door knobs from various locations throughout the country. After finding each unique door knob, I lightly restore and clean up each one by hand prior to assembling it for it's new journey as a wine stopper. Once the up-cycling process is complete, the entire crystal door knob is firmly secured to a high grade stainless steel wine stopper that will never corrode. These wine stoppers will last for many years to come and therefore that is why I have chosen to use a high grade stainless steel wine stopper rather than the less expensive chrome look alike stoppers that will eventually begin to corrode and become unusable.
